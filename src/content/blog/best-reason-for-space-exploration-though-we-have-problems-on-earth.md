Just, Imagine we are all in a cave.
**"We are caveman"**.
Now, I say that **"I would like to go out of the cave and explore what's across the vast horizon beyond the hills"**
But then you come to me and say
> **"No!!, Can't do that,
> We have problems in the cave and we need to fix the cave problems first, That has high Priority"**

and

> **"You can't go beyond the cave"**

![best reason for space exploration](/uploads/spaceexploration.jpg)

But I say,
what if the answers to our problems
is beyond the cave,
is beyond the horizon.
What if the answers to world hunger, hatred, violence and poverty is hidden deep into the Cosmos!

Well, Why are we going into the space?

* Maybe you want to know how to deflect the asteroid that would make us extinct.
* Maybe you want to tap asteroids that are rich in rare minerals and resources.
* Maybe it is because you need weather forecasting , gps, communication and Television.
* Maybe because you want to connect the world together.

Maybe to learn the Cosmos and learn that

> **"Why are we ? And What are we?"**

But, then you can say that
how would that affect you today??
Well, it won't.
There's no contribution of your existence today.
BUT we may discover something in space, that will really matter in the future.
And Maybe, maybe space exploration is mostly important because it gives you the privilege to
* Run in your athletic shoes which was made during the Apollo missions for the EVA suits for shock absorption and stability.
* Take a Cab (GPS) and wander where ever you want.
* Take a selfie which was possible due the miniaturization of cameras on spacecrafts.
* Drink Filtered water because NASA wanted to figure out how to purify water for deep space journey that gave a system which eliminated the need for frequent replacements of the cartridge used to purify the water supply.
* Get a good night's sleep as the mattress or Memory foam we sleep in , was innovated for the moon missions so that pilots are protected during the flights. today memory foams are used in beds,chairs, sofas, shoes and even in movie theatres.
* Detect and treat cancer, there's something called digital signal processing which was used for moon landings and later the technology derived into CT scanners and MRI machines which plays a major role in the medical field.

The space commands us to innovate, it motivates us to do what has never been done.
Space exploration is important because it gives you the privilege to say......

**India is the first country to find water on the Moon**

Lastly,
We can be cavemen and be safe in our cave and solve the problems inside.

Or

We can be Explorers and discover the secrets of the Nature (Cosmos) and contribute to a better Future.
