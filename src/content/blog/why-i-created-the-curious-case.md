The internet is crowded with insta pages, portfolio sites, and perfectly curated online identities for reels and shorts, basically content for the dopamine addicts. 
But I wanted something different — a place that felt cozy, personal, unfiltered, and genuinely mine.
Like something that shouts 'me' to the entire world, My own corner of anything that amuses me or anything that gives me a trigger to write/type.

![why i created the curious case of Ankan Ghosh??](/uploads/whyicreatedccag.png)

That's how **The Curious Case of Ankan Ghosh** came to life.

This website isn't just about showcasing projects or listing achievements. 
It's a collection of thoughts, experiments, ideas, and stories that don't always fit into a LinkedIn post or an Instagram caption.
Sometimes it's about technology, sometimes it's about space, books, random observations, mythology or simply questions that refuse to leave my mind and stay with me till 3AM.
I've always believed that curiosity is one of the most powerful forces we have. It pushes us to learn, create, fail, and try again. I will try to ponder about the thoughts and topics that irks me by writing my ass of on it and meanwhile figuring out what I believe, what I crave, how I stand and how the society and the world works revolving 24/7.

The question is Have you ever thought of the most unsual stuff that happen around you and couldn't figure out why, how, what or something thats so astonishing that you crave more and more about it, scour the entire internet to read on it? This blog is exactly for that purpose, to ensure the light of knowing isn't blown out from within.

This website reflects that mindset. It's not meant to be perfect or complete — it's meant to evolve, just as I do.
